Thematic Website

This website is suitable for the following kinds of readers:
1. Those who are interested in planning a study tour in Europe.
2. Those who are interested in the cultural difference between Taiwanese and Spanish.
3. Those who are interested in reading the journals of a twenty-year-old bold woman who dares to travel all by herself in a country that she had never been before.
4. Those who are forced to read these weird articles.

If you belong to those people above, welcome to my website. In these articles, I am going to share my experience and the information that I considered important for living in Europe for two months by myself.
