About the Author


I am Chiu, Chieh-Yu, a senior student majoring in foreign languages and literature at National Tsing Hua University. I was brought up in Hsinchu County and then moved to Hsinchu City when I graduated from junior high school.

Being an introvert person, I didn’t have many friends in high school. In my opinion, the easiest way to make friends is to become those who hold the events. Thus, I spent a lot of time trying to become a person who is outgoing and initiative. Eventually, I became one of the members of the student association of the department, the Care-life Club, the volleyball team, and the acapella club. However, I found that this kind of “interesting and productive” life isn’t what I wanted. Therefore, I started to spend time on things that I am interested in. I spent time practicing my drawing skills, learning how to edit video clips, and working out to keep myself fit. I found that spending time on myself instead of living someone else’s life is more meaningful to me.

I am a creative and independent person. In my spare time I am always creating something. I draw illustrations, write stories, and make music by myself. I believe that even when I am drawing a portrait, I am creating an image that stands for my observation. I hope that I can develop my hobbies of “creating something” into my sideline in the future.


我是邱捷妤，一個就讀國立清華大學外國語言學系四年級的學生。出生於新竹縣，在國中畢業後舉家搬到新竹市。（可以說是一個土生土長的新竹人）

作為一個個性較為內向的人，我在高中前沒有什麼可以交心的朋友，而我認為改變這樣情況最簡單的方式就是參與辦活動的過程。我嘗試著讓自己成為一個外向主動的人，而我也因成為了眾多社團的一份子，包括：系學會、關懷生命社、系排球隊和人生樂創社。
然而當我發現這樣「有趣且充實的生活」並不是我真正想要的時候，我才開始花時間培養自己喜歡的興趣。在那之後我開始練習插畫、學習如何剪輯影片，並嘗試透過健身讓自己保持健康的體態。也是在這時我才體會到原來為自己而活比亦步亦趨的跟著別人的腳步和觀點要來的有意義多了。

若是要我自我評價，我認為自己是一個獨立且富有創意的人。對我來說，我無時無刻都在進行創作；無論那是透過畫圖、寫作，或者製作自己的音樂，就算在我繪製人像素描時，我也在透過畫筆描繪我腦中所看見的世界。將來，我想透過這樣「創作」的過程發展副業，將工作與生活做結合。
